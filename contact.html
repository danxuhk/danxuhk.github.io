Positions

Ph.D. Students

Outstanding applicants are encouraged to apply for the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPF), which was established by Hong Kong Research Grants Council (RGC) in 2009 and aims to invite students from all over the world to pursue a Ph.D. degree in Hong Kong’s Institutions. The HKPF scheme provides pretigious and fully-funded programme for the Ph.D. studies, including extreamly attractive terms:

  • Stipend: HK$319,200 (~US$40,900) per year, for 4 years
  • Excellent Research Award: HK$40,000 (~US$5,100) in the first year of study
  • Conference Travel Allowance: HK$13,300 (~US$1,700) per year, for 4 years
  • Accommodation from the university: Guaranteed in the first 2 years of research postgraduate studies

    • More details could be found from the HKPF page of HKUST. Students interested in this scheme are warmly welcome to directly contact Prof. Xu at danxuhk@gmail.com for discussion of application, with an update-to-date CV attached and "HKPF PhD application" indicated in the subject of the inquiry email. Prof. Xu would work together with the candidates on their research proposals for the application and strongly recommends the candidates to the committee.
